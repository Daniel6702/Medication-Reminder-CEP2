Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I set up my user account?

To set up your account, visit the registration page on our web UI. You’ll need to provide personal details and medical information. Once submitted, you can configure your medication schedule and sensor settings.

How do I pair my sensors with the system?

Follow the steps in our setup guide to connect each sensor to your system via the Zigbee Gateway. Ensure each sensor is correctly positioned as detailed in the instructions.

Daily Use

What do the different colors and sounds mean?

Colors and sounds indicate your medication state: Green and a soft tone mean medication taken, red and a louder tone indicate a missed dose. Customize these in the settings menu.

How do I reschedule a medication?

To adjust medication times, log into your web UI, go to the medication schedule section, and update the times as needed.

Troubleshooting

What should I do if the sensor doesn’t detect medication intake?

If the sensor fails to detect an event, you can manually enter the medication intake via the web UI. Check sensor battery levels and positioning if issues persist.

How do I handle a low battery alert for a sensor?

When you receive a low battery notification, replace the sensor's battery as soon as possible to ensure continuous monitoring.

Device Information and Configuration

How can I change the audio and visual alerts for medication reminders?

You can customize alerts through the web UI under ‘Alert Settings’. Choose colors, sounds, and even alert patterns according to your preference.

Security and Privacy

What security measures are in place to protect my data?

All data is encrypted using advanced security protocols. Regular audits are performed to ensure that our security measures are up to date.

Technical Support

Where can I find more help if I encounter issues?

Visit the ‘Help’ section in the web UI for tutorials and troubleshooting tips. For further assistance, contact our support team via the provided contact details.
